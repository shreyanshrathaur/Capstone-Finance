Our mission is to simplify finance for everyone.

We believe that managing your money, from everyday payments to long-term investments, should be simple, transparent, and accessible to all.

The FinPay Story

FinPay was founded in 2024 by a group of Newton School of Technology students who were frustrated with the complex and fragmented financial landscape. Traditional banking apps were clunky, investment platforms were intimidating, and simple payments were riddled with hidden fees.

We knew there had to be a better way.

Our goal was to build a single, beautiful, and powerful platform that put the user first. A platform that combined the best of payments, investing, and credit into one seamless experience. This capstone project is the first step on that journey.

Built with modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, FinPay represents our passion for problem-solving and our commitment to building tools that genuinely help people.
